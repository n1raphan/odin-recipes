Pad Kra Pao

A true Thai classic dish!

Ingredients for Pad Kra Pao

  • Holy basil or regular basil
  • Chili
  • Garlic
  • Fish sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Vegetable oil
  • Any meat of your choice

How to make Pad Kra Pao

Add the oil and grounded garlic and chili to a hot wok or skillet and stir until fragrant. Then add the meat and cook it briefly for a couple minutes.

Next, add a teaspoon of fish sauce and soy sauce or salt and stir fry for a few seconds. Finally, add the holy basil and mix it together and then turn off the heat
